Why I Want to Be a Front-End Developer and How HNG Will Help Me Achieve My Goals

I am Ugorji Eze Victor from Rivers state, Nigeria. I am an aspiring front-end developer currently enrolled in the HNG 12 internship with the aim of building my skills and solving some real world problems to be industry ready.

My Motivation for Becoming a Front-End Developer

Technology is the driving force of transformation in the world over. I am motivated by a creative expression driven by technology to solve problems with technology. My passion for creativity and problem-solving are some of the motivations for my desire to pursue a career as a front-end developer. I find excitement in creating visually appealing designs, user-friendly interfaces, interactive user interfaces that improve user experience. I understand the demand for front-end developers in the technology industry, hence, I am driven to pursue a career in the tech industry as a frontend developer.

How HNG Will Help Me Grow in the Field

HNG 12 internship offers the opportunity for me to build my proficiency through tasks and mentorships, it will help me learn and master technologies, frameworks required in the industry, it will help me measure myself and skills against industry standard to see where I fall-short, HNG internship pools a community of developers, offering the opportunity to network and collaborate with others thereby accelerating my growth and by solving real world problems, build my experience and communication which are key to a successful tech career. Participating in HNG internship will provide the opportunity to meet potential employers who are looking to HIRE RECT.JS DEVELOPERS as well firms who have the need to HIRE WEB DEVELOPERS, increasing my chances of landing a job in the industry.

My Goals for the Internship and How I Plan to Achieve

  • Master Front-End Frameworks:

    Through HNG internship, I intend to master frontend frameworks such as ReactJs and NextJs by solving real-world problems. This I will achieve through active participation in tasks, assigned projects and feedback from mentors.

  • Improve Problem-Solving Skills:

    Understanding algorithms and improving performance are some skills I intend to master. Taking advantage of tasks, I intend to practice my way to proficiency.

  • Enhance Collaboration and Communication:

    Contrary to what I am used to, HNG internship provides the opportunity to collaborate which is a normal industry practice. This I plan to improve on through communication with my fellow interns, participation in discussions and and through contribution to group projects.

  • Certification:

    Certifications make up for experience, hence I plan to earn my certification through participation and commitment to project completion.

  • Portfolio:

    By the end of the internship, I aim to have a well-developed portfolio showcasing my skills and projects. This will serve as a testament to my skills and make me a strong candidate for job opportunities.

  • Network with Industry Professionals:

    Connecting with experienced developers and potential employers will help me stay informed about industry trends and job opportunities. I will actively engage in networking events, webinars, and discussions within the HNG community to achieve this goal.
